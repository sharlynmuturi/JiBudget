What is JiBudget?

JiBudget is a smart budgeting app built for all income types. Whether you earn daily, weekly, or monthly, it helps you track income, manage spending, and stay intentional with your money - across all your wallets, all in one place.

Money made simple, for everyone

Good money management shouldn’t be complicated. That's why JiBudget makes it simple, visual, and personal - so you can budget with ease, stay on track, and feel confident about your financial future.

Transactions

Record and review your spending

Get a clear view of what you earn, spend, and when, all in one searchable, up-to-date place. Record your recurring transactions in a single click. Bring your finances to life with clear, beautiful charts.

Budgets

Plan ahead for smarter spending

Set monthly budgets for your spending categories, log expenses as they happen. JiBudget tracks progress automatically. Easily create this month’s budget by reusing previous months'.

Wallets

See where your money lives

Whether it’s cash, mobile money, or bank accounts. Log every transaction to the right wallet and get a clear view of what’s come in, what’s gone out, and what’s left.

Loan Payoff simulator

Stay on Top of Your Borrowing

Keep track of all your loans in one place. See what’s left to pay, and explore simulations to see how extra payments can help you save time and interest.

Investments

Track Your Investment Portfolio

Keep tabs on all your investments - chamas, SACCOs, MMF, stocks, and more, all in one place. See the latest stock prices, watch interactive charts, and know exactly how your funds are performing. Stay informed, make smarter decisions, and grow your wealth with ease.

Money Vitals

Your Financial Health Check

Get a quick read on your financial health - how long your money can last, how much is liquid, and whether your spending matches your income. Record every income and expense as it happens for the clearest picture.
